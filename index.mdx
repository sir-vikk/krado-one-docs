## What is Krado One?

**Krado One** is a self-hosted internal developer platform built with React + Vite, Go + Gin, and SQLite or PostgreSQL.

It unifies all your engineering workflows in one place — tasks, incidents, service catalog, runbooks, cloud visibility, security, and more.
