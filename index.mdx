## Up and running in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pull the Docker image">
    One compose file. No build tooling, no Node or Go runtime needed on your host.
    ```bash
    curl -o docker-compose.yml https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sir-vikk/krado-one/main/deploy/docker/docker-compose.yml
    docker compose up -d
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the setup wizard">
    Create your org and owner account in under 60 seconds on first launch at **http://localhost**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure integrations">
    Connect Jira, Slack, GitHub, and cloud providers via **Portal Config → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Add members, assign roles, and enable the portals your team needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
