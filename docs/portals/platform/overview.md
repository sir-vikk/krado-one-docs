# Platform Engineering Overview

The Platform Engineering Portal consolidates the tooling your platform team needs into a single workspace. From technology adoption tracking to CI/CD visibility, change management, and developer onboarding, everything is accessible from one place.

## Available Features

### [Tech Radar](./tech-radar.md)

Track your organization's technology adoption stance across languages, platforms, tools, and techniques. Classify each technology into Adopt, Trial, Assess, or Hold rings to guide engineering decisions.

### [CI/CD Pipelines](./pipelines.md)

View a consolidated history of CI/CD pipeline runs across all services. Monitor success rates, average build durations, and identify frequently failing services.

### [API Catalog](./api-catalog.md)

Maintain an internal registry of all HTTP APIs your services expose. Track versions, ownership, documentation links, and deprecation timelines.

### [Change Requests](./change-requests.md)

Manage a lightweight approval workflow for significant infrastructure or platform changes before they are implemented. Track reviews, approvals, and implementation status.

### [Environment Promotions](./promotions.md)

Follow the lifecycle of changes as they move through dev, staging, and production environments. Each promotion records approval state and maintains a full audit trail.

### [Status Page](./status-page.md)

Track the operational status of your user-facing services and provide a view that customers or internal teams can check during incidents.

### [Developer Onboarding](./onboarding.md)

Create structured onboarding checklists for new developers, assign them to team members, and track completion progress across your organization.
